Summary for New Players
Start the Game:

The game starts with Player 1 as the active player.
Player's Turn:

Roll the Dice: Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.
If the dice shows a number other than 1, that number is added to your current score.
If the dice shows a 1, your turn ends, you lose your current score, and it's the next player's turn.
Hold the Score: Click the "Hold" button to add your current score to your total score. Your turn ends, and it's the next player's turn.
Winning the Game:

The first player to reach 50 points in their total score wins the game.
New Game:

Click the "New Game" button to reset the game and start over.
This game is simple and easy to play, but it requires strategy on when to hold your score and when to risk rolling the dice again. Enjoy playing!
